Our Story, Our Commitment

For over five decades, we've cultivated more than oil palms – we've nurtured communities, protected ecosystems, and built a legacy of sustainable excellence.

Our Journey

Five decades of growth, innovation, and sustainable leadership

1972
Humble Beginnings
Founded as a modest 500-hectare plantation in Johor, Malaysia, with just 50 employees and a vision for quality palm oil production.
1985
First Palm Oil Mill
Commissioned our first mill, processing 60,000 MT annually and marking our entry into integrated operations.
1998
Regional Expansion
Expanded operations to Sabah and Sarawak, increasing planted area to 50,000 hectares.
2006
Public Listing
Listed on Bursa Malaysia, providing access to capital for further growth and modernization.
2015
Sustainability Milestone
Achieved 100% MSPO certification across all plantations, committing to zero-deforestation practices.
2024
Global Recognition
Recognized as one of Malaysia's top 10 sustainable palm oil producers, with exports to 25+ countries.
