About Egbeude Plant & Yield Ltd

Agricultural & Real Estate company focused on oil palm plantation and other crops, based in Abia State, Nigeria.

Who We Are

Building a legacy in Nigerian agriculture and real estate

Rooted in Abia State

Egbeude Plant & Yield Ltd is an Agricultural and Real Estate Company that primarily focuses on oil palm plantation and other crops. The company has been in business for one year before being registered in October 2025 with the Corporate Affairs Commission (CAC).

We manage a 30-hectare oil palm plantation in Nkporo community, Ohafia Local Government Area, Abia State, and maintain our corporate headquarters at N117 Cameron Road, Aba, Abia State, Nigeria.

  • 30+ hectares under cultivation
  • CAC registered since October 2025
  • 1 year of pre-registration operations
  • Oil palm + real estate diversification
