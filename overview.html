Professional Plantation Management

Our operations are centered on a 30-hectare oil palm plantation in Nkporo community, Ohafia Local Government Area, Abia State. With one year of pre-registration experience, we have refined our farming systems to deliver quality fresh fruit bunches while maintaining ecological balance.

  • 30+ hectares under active cultivation
  • Strategic location in Ohafia LGA, Abia State
  • High-yield oil palm varieties
  • 100% traceable local supply chain
