Welcome to Egbeude Plant & Yiled

As a leading Malaysian plantation company, we specialize in sustainable oil palm cultivation and palm oil milling. With decades of experience in agricultural development, we combine traditional expertise with modern innovation to deliver excellence in plantation management.

Sustainability & Corporate Responsibility

At Egbeude Plant & Yiled, sustainability is at the heart of everything we do. We are committed to responsible plantation management that protects the environment while supporting local communities.

Our sustainability framework focuses on three key pillars: environmental stewardship, social responsibility, and economic viability. We ensure all our operations meet international standards for sustainable palm oil production.
