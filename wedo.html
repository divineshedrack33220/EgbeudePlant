Oil Palm Plantation

Our flagship operation — 30 hectares of productive farmland in Nkporo

High-Yield Oil Palm Production

Our 30-hectare oil palm plantation is located in Nkporo community, Ohafia Local Government Area, Abia State. We focus on producing quality fresh fruit bunches (FFB) through sustainable and responsible farming methods.

  • 30 hectares of productive oil palm
  • Located in Nkporo, Ohafia LGA, Abia State
  • Quality fresh fruit bunch production
  • Sustainable farming practices

Diverse Crops & Food Security

Beyond oil palm — contributing to Nigeria's agricultural diversity

Intercropping & Crop Diversification

We practice intercropping alongside our oil palm cultivation, growing other agricultural products that enhance food security and provide additional revenue. This approach maximizes land utilization and supports local food systems.

  • Intercropping for optimal land use
  • Supporting food security in Abia State
  • Diverse agricultural products
  • Sustainable farming methods

Strategic Land & Property Investments

In addition to our agricultural focus, Egbeude Plant & Yield Ltd engages in strategic real estate investments. Our corporate headquarters is located at N117 Cameron Road, Aba, Abia State, and we continue to explore property opportunities that align with our growth strategy.

  • Strategic property investments
  • Complementing agricultural assets
  • Long-term value creation
